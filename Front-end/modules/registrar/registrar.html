Students

Columns mirror the Enrollment Form (sections 1–6). Use dot-paths in headers — e.g. learner.firstName, father.mobileNumber, emergency.fullName.
Required: enrollmentDate, schoolYear, program, gradeLevel, learner.lastName, learner.firstName, learner.birthDate (YYYY-MM-DD), learner.gender, emergency.fullName, emergency.relationship, emergency.mobileNumber, emergency.homeAddress, and at least one parent (Father or Mother) with last/first name, address, and mobile number.
Conditional: if other.shuttleService is yes, then other.carpoolService (none / morning / afternoon) is required.
Optional: learner.middleName, learner.schoolLastAttended, other.shuttleService, other.escGrantee, parent middleName / religion / telephoneNumber.
Headers are matched loosely — “Father First Name”, “f_firstname”, “Date of Birth”, etc. all work. Required documents (PSA, Report Card, etc.) are not imported here — attach them per student afterwards.
